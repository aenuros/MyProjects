Pig Latin

Welcome to the Ace Linguist Pig Latin translator! This translator will take a single sentence and "translate" it to Pig Latin. Please note this translator has some limitations:

  1. It only works on one sentence at a time.
  2. It does not work with punctation, including quotation marks.
  3. It does not preserve capitalization, so everything comes out lower-case.

I am working on improving the Pig Latin translator so that you can translate without limitations.
